# Prompt: Hook Checker

Evaluate only the first two seconds of a TikTok Shop video.

Score curiosity, problem clarity, native phrasing, silent readability, product visibility, and scroll-stopping power. Classify the hook as strong, average, weak, or needs rewrite.

Return five safe alternatives: curiosity, problem, relatable confession, visual demonstration, and light challenge. Keep each line speakable within two seconds. Avoid robotic advertising language, unsupported superlatives, and fake urgency.
