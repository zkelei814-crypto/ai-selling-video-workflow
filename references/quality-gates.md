# Quality gates

## Automatic before rendering

- Every spoken product claim maps to a usable fact-ledger item.
- The timeline starts at 0, ends at the requested duration, and has no gaps or overlaps.
- The product appears in the hook and is used by second five.
- Voiceover fits natural speech for the duration.
- Captions are short and marked for safe-center placement.
- The CTA has no fake urgency.
- The prompt contains identity locks and negative constraints.
- Generated copy is relevant to the supplied product rather than a leftover template category.
- Hazardous tools include appropriate PPE, a secured workpiece, a clear cutting path, powered-off handling, and no tool near a face, child, or pet.

## Manual after rendering

- Product shape, color, controls, packaging, scale, and brand spelling match references.
- Hands, faces, product parts, and text have no visible artifacts.
- Demonstration is physically plausible and safe.
- Captions match the voiceover and remain inside safe margins.
- Audio is synchronized and intelligible.
- No extra accessories, invented claims, or unsafe behavior appear.

Fail the asset when any identity, safety, claim, or synchronization check fails. Revise the storyboard or prompt, not the approved facts.
